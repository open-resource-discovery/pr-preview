CSN Interop Annotations

This section describes aligned and interoperable annotations, that are provided along with the core CSN Interop specification.

The annotations documented here are a curated subset of existing annotation vocabularies in the SAP ecosystem, that we deem important for interoperability.

@Aggregation annotations.
